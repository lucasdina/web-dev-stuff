7 Steps Every Cancer Patient Should Take

1 Lean on friends that can see this through. 2 Seek as much knowledge as possible. 3 Treat your doctor and care givers as your best allies. 4 If the situation is grave, get a second and third opinion. 5 Embrace your treatment plan. This is how you will get well. 6 Be prepared to change. 7 Fill your body with good food and your mind with positive thoughts.
